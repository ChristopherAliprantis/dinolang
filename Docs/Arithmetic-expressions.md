
**+(2 nums or strings or more)**

example: print(+(7, 8));<br>
output: 15<br>
example: print(+(:2:, :8:, :!:));<br>

**-(2 nums or more)**

example: print(-(8, 9, 8.678));<br>

**/(2 nums or more)**

example: print(/(4, 2));<br>

***(2 nums or more)**

**%(2 nums or more)**
