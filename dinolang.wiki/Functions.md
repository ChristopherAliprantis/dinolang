
**Print**

print(func());

**Assign Var To**

var = func();

**Declare**

#func func();

**Return**

return(:Hello, World:);

returns can be empty
**End**

#endfunc;

**Example**

NOTE: you should usually have 4 spaces at the beggining of a function's line for clear code, but you can't see it on here because of the markdown renderer

#func str5();<br>
   return(:5:);<br>
#endfunc;<br>

print(str5());

output:

5
